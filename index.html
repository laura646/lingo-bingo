How it works

It's easy. Really.

🎫

1.Show up

Come to Ilik Cafe and check in at the door. That's the only hard part — and it's not hard at all!

🏳️

2.Get your flags

We'll give you a little flag for each language you can speak, and each one you'd like to get better at.

📌

3.Wear them

Pin your flags on, so people can see your languages at a glance — no need to explain.

💬

4.Start chatting

Order a drink, find someone with an interesting flag, and say hello. That's it — you're in!

Who is it for?

Everyone who loves languages — and everyone who'd like to. 🎉 You don't have to be perfect or fluent: complete beginners and confident speakers are equally welcome. Come on your own or bring a friend — either way, you'll meet plenty of new people. No lessons, no pressure… just good chats and good company.

Lingo Bingo is brought to you by English with Laura — a language school that helps people speak with real confidence. We believe you learn a language best by actually using it with other people, so we created this night to bring language lovers in Yerevan together for a fun, relaxed evening.
